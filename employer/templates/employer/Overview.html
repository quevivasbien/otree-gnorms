In this study, you will play the role of an employer evaluating job applicants. The “job applicants” are other Mturkers who participated in a different version of the study.

This study has three parts:

  • In Part 1, you will decide the maximum amount you are willing to pay to job applicants to buy their performance in a test. You can earn a bonus from this part depending on your responses and on the performance of the applicants you review.
  • In Parts 2 and 3, you will guess the performance of the applicants and the social appropriateness of how they evaluate themselves in their "job application portfolios." You can earn a bonus from these parts if you make accurate guesses.

You will review the profiles of up to 30 applicants in each part of the study.

By completing this study, you will receive a guaranteed payment of ${{ Constants.payment }} within 24 hours. You will also receive a bonus payment based on your responses in a randomly-selected part out of the three parts. This bonus payment will be up to ${{ Constants.max_bonus }} and will be paid to you within a week.
