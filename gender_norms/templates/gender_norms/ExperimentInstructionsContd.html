On the next screens, you will read the description of a situation in which a person named {{ player.name }} must make a decision. You will read about the decisions {{ player.name }} faced and what actions {{ player.pronoun }} has to choose between.

Your job is to rate {{ player.name }}’s decision based on whether you think the decision is

“socially appropriate”

and

“consistent with what most people who are like you think that {{ player.name }} OUGHT to do.”

By “socially appropriate”, we mean behavior that most people agree is “reasonable” or “correct,” and consistent with proper social behavior in that context; it is what {{ player.name }} “ought” to do in that context. Another way to think about what we mean is that if {{ player.name }} were to select a socially inappropriate choice, then someone might be angry at {{ player.pronoun_object }} or form a negative impression of {{ player.pronoun_object }} for doing so, thinking for example {{ player.name }} is inconsiderate, rude, arrogant, timid etc., depending on the situation.

That sounds simple, but it is only half the story!

You will only earn the bonus if your “social appropriateness” rating MATCHES the rating of another Mturker working on this HIT today who is like you (i.e., a US resident who has completed at least 100 tasks on MTurk with a 95% or better approval rating from prior MTurk employers). We will call this Mturker “your match.”

We will match you with another Mturker who is like you. To increase the chances that you earn the bonus, you should try to imagine what your match, who is like you, would say.

You will also answer what your personal opinion is about the appropriateness of {{ player.name }}’s decision.

On the next screens, you will read about the situation that {{ player.name }} is in and the decisions that {{ player.pronoun }} makes. The description will include possible actions available to {{ player.name }}.

Your task is to rate {{ player.name }}’s decision based on your guess of whether your MATCH, who is like you, would think the decision was “socially appropriate” and “consistent with what someone like you would think {{ player.name }} OUGHT to do.”

Remember that you will only earn bonus if your “social appropriateness” rating is the same as your MATCH’s rating. For each rating that is the same, you will earn 20 cents (and so will your match).

You will also answer what your personal opinion is about the appropriateness of {{ player.name }}’s decision. You DO NOT have to match your answer with a MATCH for this and will not get a bonus for this question.
