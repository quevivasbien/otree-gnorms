This is the last stage of the experiment!

In this part, you will see different versions of your job application portfolio and of other applicants' portfolios. For each portfolio you see, you will:

  • guess the highest amount that a randomly chosen employer would pay for that portfolio.
  • guess a randomly chosen employer's belief about the number of job performance questions solved by the applicant in that portfolio.
  • guess a randomly chosen employer's belief about the social appropriateness of the applicant's self-evaluation in that portfolio.

One of your guesses will be randomly chosen, and if it is close to the value actually chosen by a randomly selected employer who sees that portfolio, your bonus payment for Part 3 will be $1.00.
