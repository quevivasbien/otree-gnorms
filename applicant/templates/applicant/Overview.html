In this study, you will play the role of a job applicant.

This study has three parts, meant to simulate a job interview process. You have an opportunity for a bonus payment in each part.

  • In Part 1, you will take a cognitive ability test consisting of 20 questions as part of the job interview process; the bonus for Part 1 depends on how well you do in these questions.
  • In Part 2, you create your job application portfolio. Based on this portfolio, employers will bid on a wage to pay you for your work in Part 1. The highest bid will be your Part 2 bonus.
  • In Part 3, you will make a guess about what wage the employers will offer you and other Mturkers with different job application portfolios. The bonus for Part 3 depends on how accurately you make these guesses.

For completing this study, you are guaranteed to receive $X within 24 hours. You will also receive a bonus payment based on your performance in one randomly selected part out of the three parts. This bonus payment will be up to $X.
