If this part is the selected part for your bonus payment, the bonus given to you and your employer will be determined as follows:

Your bonus will equal the highest wage offered to you among all the employer offers. The employer with the highest wage offer will get a bonus of (i) $1.00 minus the wage offered to you plus (ii) 10 cents times the number of questions you answered correctly in the ten “job performance” questions. The potential employers who did not make the highest wage offer will get a bonus of $1.00.
