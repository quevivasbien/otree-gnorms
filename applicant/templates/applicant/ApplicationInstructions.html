How is your bonus determined?

Your application portfolio will be reviewed by “potential employers,” who are MTurk workers similar to you and complete a different version of this study. They will look at your portfolio and decide the amount (1 to 100 cents) they are willing to pay in order to receive 10 cents for each job performance question you correctly answered in Part 1. (Remember that the job performance questions are the other 10 questions beside theapplication questions used to determine your bonus for Part 1.)

One of those employers will be randomly chosen, and the amount that he/she is willing to pay for your job performance questions will be your bonus for Part 2.
