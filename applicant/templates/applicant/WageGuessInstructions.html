This is the last stage of the experiment!

In this part you answer questions in which you will

  • guess the highest wage you would be offered for your portfolio by the employers.
  • guess the highest wage other MTurkers would be offered for their portfolios by the employers.

One of these questions will be randomly chosen, and if your guess in that question is within 5 cents of the actual highest wage offer, then your bonus payment for this part will be $1.00.

This is how your waged will be determined: five employers will see your portfolio and will bid on the maximum wage they are willing to pay for your “job performance” questions; whoever bids the highest will be your employer, and the wage you get will be their bid. The employers can bid between 0 and 100 cents.

Remember that the employer who hires you will see only the information in your portfolio and will receive 10 cents for every “job performance” question you correctly answered.
