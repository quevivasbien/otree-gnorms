In this stage, you will complete a test made up of 20 questions from the Armed Services Vocational Aptitude Battery (ASVAB), with four questions from each of the following five categories: general science, arithmetic reasoning, math knowledge, mechanical comprehension, and assembling objects.

You will be shown the questions one at a time and will have up to 30 seconds to answer each question, although you can click Next on each question to move on before the 30 seconds are up.

We will randomly select 10 out of the 20 questions you answer, and if this stage is the stage selected for your bonus payment, you will receive a bonus payment of 10 cents times the number of those questions that you answer correctly, in addition to your base payment of $X.

You can see some example questions below.
