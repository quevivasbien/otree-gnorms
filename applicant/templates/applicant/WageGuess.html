On the following three screens, you will see different versions of the job application portfolio you created in Part 2.

For each version of your portfolio, your task is to guess the amount that an employer who sees that portfolio would be willing to pay in exchange for 10 cents times the number of job performance questions you correctly answered.

The employers will see only the information presented in each portfolio, plus the average number of application questions correctly answered by all applicants, which is {{ Constants.mean_performance }} out of 10.

One of the portfolios will be selected to determine your bonus payment: if your guess on that portfolio is within 5 cents of the amount that a randomly selected employer would pay for the portfolio, your bonus payment will be $1.00.
